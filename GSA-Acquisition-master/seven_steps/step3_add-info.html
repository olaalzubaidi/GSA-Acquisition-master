Additional Information

If you would like to learn more about market research, we recommend the following sites and publications:

Relevant Regulations

   Federal Acquisition Regulation, http://www.acquisition.gov/far/loadmainre.html
especially:

FAR Part 10, Market Research
prescribes policies and procedures for conducting market research to arrive at the most suitable approach to acquiring, distributing, and supporting supplies and services

Websites about Market Research

   A "Business Researcher's Jumpstation"
This complex website has loads of links to explore and is billed as "The Premier Business and Technology Portal and Global Community Network for E-Business, Information, Technology, and Knowledge Management recommended by Business Week, Fortune, Wall Street Journal, Fast Company, Computerworld, Information Week, CIO Magazine, KM World, and, Harvard Business School Publishing."

   MIT Mechanical Engineering Resource List
This site is not just limited to mechanical engineering.
