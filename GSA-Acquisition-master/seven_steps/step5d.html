H.2.4 Changes to the Award Term Plan

Either the Contractor or the Government may initiate changes to the award term plan. Bilateral changes may be made to the award term plan at any time during contract performance. The Government may make unilateral changes to the award term plan before the beginning of a new award term evaluation period. Such changes to the award term plan will be provided at least 30 days prior to the start of the new evaluation period. Such changes shall not entitle the Contractor to any equitable adjustments or to any other compensation.
