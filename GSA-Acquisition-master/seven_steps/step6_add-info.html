Relevant Regulations

   Federal Acquisition Regulation, http://acquisition.gov/far/loadmainre.html especially:

FAR Part 9, Contractor Qualifications, addresses past performance as one factor in determining contractor responsibility.

FAR Subpart 9.5 , Organizational and Consultant Conflicts of Interest.

FAR 15.102 addresses using oral presentations in negotiated procurements.

FAR Part 15.304 addresses past performance as an evaluation factor in negotiated procurements.

FAR Part 42 , Contract Administration, addresses the collection of contractor performance information during the performance of the contract
