CONOPS Tools--Project Planning
An Example of Typical Tasks Performed
by an Acquisition Project Team
ID Task Actual Time Depends on Who is Responsible
1        
2 INITIATE PROJECT      
3 Earmark the new project in the agency's Strategic Plan, IT Plan and Budget 2h   Program Manager
4 Identify appropriate project sponsor with which to negotiate the Project Agreement 2h 3 Program Manager
5 Identify the project team leader 1h 3 Program Manager
6 Brief program manager, project sponsor and team leader on principles of CONOPS 3h 4,5 Transition Team
7        
8 FORM PROJECT TEAM      
9 Identify types of skills needed on the project team 2h 5,6 Team Leader, Transition Team
10 Identify actual team members and advisors 8h 9 Team Leader, Project Sponsor
11 Clarify roles, responsibilities, commitment requirements with team member's supervisors 8h 10 Team Leader, Project Sponsor
12 Provide CONOPS kickoff training to project team (including advisors) 2h 10 Transition Team
13 Provide basic teamwork training to project team (including advisors) 24h 10 Outside Training Resource
14 Establish project team ground rules, roles and responsibilities 8h 12,13 Team (including Advisors)
15 Complete any required COTR training 40h 14 Designated COTR
16 Provide ethics briefing to project team 2h 10 Office of General Counsel
17 Brief project team on procurement integrity 1h 10 Office of General Counsel
18 Prepare and maintain a team acquisition schedule 1h 14 Designated Team Member
19 Establish project's secured Intranet website 8h 14 Web Technician
20 Establish project's public Internet website 8h 14 Web Technician
21        
22 DEVELOP PROJECT AGREEMENT      
23 Provide Project Agreement training to project team 2h 12,13 Transition Team
24 Develop Project Agreement 24h 23 Team (including Advisors)
25 Team sign off on Project Agreement 1h 24 Team
26 Present Project Agreement to Project Sponsor 2h 25 Team (including Advisors)
27 Project Sponsor sign off on Project Agreement 8h 26 Project Sponsor
28 Prepare publication version of Project Agreement (and any supporting documents) 2h 27 Web Technician

30 PREPARE FOR MARKET RESEARCH      
31 Provide market research training to project team 2h 27 Transition Team
32 Provide Statement of Need training to team (optional at this time) 2h 31 Transition Team
33 Develop market research Request for Information and Ground Rules 16h 31 Team (including Advisors)
34 Identify relevant Federal Acquisition Regulation clauses 4h 33 Contracting Officer
35 Develop draft Statement of Need (optional at this time) 24h 32 Team
36 Final legal review of Request for Information Package 8h 33,34,35 Legal Advisor
37 Prepare publication version of Request for Information Package 2h 36 Web Technician
38 Prepare CBD announcement for Project Agreement and Request for Information 2h 36 Contracting Officer
39 Announce Project Agreement and Request for Information in CBDNet 1h 38 Contracting Officer
40 Publish Project Agreement and Request for Information on the public Internet (no wait) 1h 39 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
41 Develop and maintain "Interested Parties" list on public Internet 4h 40 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
42 Develop and maintain "Project Status" report on public Internet 2h 40 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
43        
44 CONDUCT DIALOG WITH VENDORS (MARKET RESEARCH PHASE)      
45 Form Answer Subteam 2h 40 Team
46 Develop ground rules for answering vendor questions 2h 45 Answer Subteam
47 Receive vendor questions via email 0d 40 Contracting Officer, Answer Subteam
48 Prepare responses to vendors' questions 0d 47 Answer Subteam, Contracting Officer
49 Provide legal clearance of responses to vendors' questions (when appropriate) 0d 48 Legal Advisor
50 Post answers to vendors' questions on the public Internet (ongoing or in batches) 1h 49 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
51        
52 IDENTIFY VIABLE ALTERNATIVES (MARKET RESEARCH PHASE)      
53 Close Request for Information and Questions/Answers Phase 1h 40 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
54 Receive vendor approaches in response to Request for Information 1h 53 Contracting Officer
55 Review vendor approaches for viable alternatives (assuming 10 approaches received) 80h 54 Team
56 Hold discussions with firms that submitted substantive comments (optional) 40h 55 Team
57 Select and document viable alternatives 24h 55,56 Team
58 Hold project sponsor briefing - market research results 2h 57 Team
59        
60 PREPARE REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS      
61 Provide evaluation approaches training to project team 3h 57 Transition Team
62 Develop/finalize evaluation criteria and Statement of Need 80h 32,61 Team
63 Develop draft Evaluation Plan 16h 62 Team
64 Develop Ground Rules for offerer participation in proposal process 8h 62,63 Team
65 Identify relevant Federal Acquisition Regulation clauses 4h 62,63 Contracting Officer
66 Consolidate parts into the Request for Proposals 2h 62,63,64 Contracting Officer
67 Final legal review of Request for Proposals package 8h 66 Legal Advisor
68 Hold project sponsor briefing - RFP plan 2h 67 Team
69 Prepare Request for Proposals CBD announcement 2h 62,64 Contracting Officer
70 Announce Request for Proposals in CBDNet (usually for 15 days) 15ed 69 Contracting Officer
71 Prepare publication version of Request for Proposals 16h 67 Web Technician
72 Publish Request for Proposals on the public Internet (usually for 30 days) 30ed 71 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
73 Prepare any required amendments to the Request for Proposals 0d 72 Team
74 Final legal review of amendments to the Request for Proposals (when appropriate) 0d 73 Legal Advisor
75 Post amendments to the RFP on the public Internet (may extend wait period) 0d 74 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
76        
77 CONDUCT DIALOG WITH VENDORS (RFP Phase)      
78 Periodically update "project status" report on the public Internet 1h 72 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
79 Receive vendor questions via email 1h 72 Answer Subteam, Contracting Officer
80 Prepare responses to vendor questions 8h 79 Answer Subteam, Contracting Officer
81 Provide legal clearance of answers to vendor questions (when appropriate) 2h 80 Legal Advisor
82 Post answers to vendor questions on the public Internet (ongoing or in batches) 1h 81 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
83 Hold site visits (if applicable) 24h 72 Contracting Officer, Selected Team Members
84 Close questions/answers phase (usually 2 weeks prior to proposal due date) 1h 72 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
85        
86 PREPARE FOR EVALUATION OF PROPOSALS      
87 Prepare secured evaluation subteam work space 8h 72 Team
88 Identify evaluation subteam 2h 72 Team
89 Train evaluation subteam on methodology, roles, responsibilities 2h 88 Transition Team
90 Train evaluation subteam on oral presentations (if planned) 2h 88 Transition Team
91 Provide ethics briefing to new members on the evaluation subteam 2h 88 Office of General Counsel
92 Brief new evaluation subteam members on procurement integrity 2h 88 Office of General Counsel
93 Prepare past performance survey questionnaire 16h 62,63 Team
94 Prepare oral presentation instructions to offerers (if planned) 4h 62,63 Contracting Officer
95 Schedule documentation method for oral presentations (if planned) 4h 62,63 Selected Team Members
96        
97 EVALUATE PROPOSALS      
98 Receive written proposals 8h 72 Contracting Officer
99 Evaluate written proposals 40h 98 Evaluation Subteam
100 Check past performance and other relevant criteria 24h 98 Evaluation Subteam, Contracting Officer
101 Check Representations and Certifications 8h 98 Contracting Officer
102 Deliver oral presentation instructions to offerers (if planned) 1h 94,98 Contracting Officer
103 Schedule oral presentations with offerers 2h 102 Contracting Officer
104 Hold oral presentations/evaluations (if planned - assuming 10 presentations) 80h 103 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
105 Determine competitive range and document decision (optional at this time) 16h 104 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam
106 Notify down-selected offerers 4h 105 Contracting Officer
107 Conduct debriefings if requested (assuming 5 debriefings requested) 15h 106 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
108 Schedule live test demonstrations (if planned) 2h 105 Contracting Officer
109 Finalize travel itineraries 4h 108 Selected Team Members
110 Schedule travel and accommodations 4h 109 Selected Team Members
111 Conduct live test demonstrations (assuming 5 LTDs) 80h 110 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
112 Determine competitive range and document decision (optional at this time) 8h 111 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam
113 Notify down-selected offerers 2h 112 Contracting Officer
114 Conduct debriefings if requested (assuming 2 debriefings requested) 6h 113 Contracting Officer,Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
115        
116 NEGOTIATE CONTRACT      
117 Develop instructions for preparing proposed performance-based model contract 8h 98 Contracting Officer, Selected Team members,Legal Advisor
118 Issue instructions to offerors for preparing performance-based model contract 2h 117 Contracting Officer
119 Receive revisions to proposal and performance-based model contract from offerers 2h 118 Contracting Officer
120 Review model contract, develop questions/comments and reach consensus (round 1) 48h 119 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam
121 Conduct clarifications/discussions/negotiations (round 1) 24h 120 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
122 Receive revisions to proposal and performance-based model contract from offerers (round 2) 2h 121 Contracting Officer
123 Review model contract, develop questions/comments and reach consensus (round 2) 48h 122 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam
124 Conduct clarifications/discussions/negotiations (round 2) 24h 123 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
125 Receive best and final offers (BAFOs) (optional) 2h 124 Contracting Officer
126 Review BAFOs, determine best value, make source selection and document decision 24h 125 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam
127        
128 AWARD CONTRACT      
129 Issue CD-435 4h 126 Team Leader, Budget Advisor
130 Hold project sponsor preaward briefing 2h 126 Team
131 Hold organizational/congressional briefings (if appropriate) 8h 130 Team, Project Sponsor
132 Issue press release (if appropriate) 4h 130 Team, Project Sponsor
133 Sign contract 2h 126 Contracting Officer, Winning Offerer
134 Prepare award announcement 1h 133 Contracting Officer
135 Post award announcement on CBDNet and project Internet 1h 134 Contracting Officer, Web Technician
136 Conduct final debriefings as requested 8h 133 Contracting Officer, Evaluation Subteam, Offerers
137 Celebrate award 4h 133 Team (including Advisors)
