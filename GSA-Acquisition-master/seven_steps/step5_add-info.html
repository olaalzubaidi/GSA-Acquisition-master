Websites about Performance Measurement and Management

   National Academy of Public Administration web page, Center for Improving Government Performance This site includes links websites related to GPRA, outcome measures, and performance measurement.

   "Performance-Based Management Handbook: A Six-Volume Compilation of Tools and Techniques for Implementing the Government Performance and Results Act of 1993," developed by a Department of Energy special interest group.

A comprehensive information site for establishing and maintaining a performance-based program, including performance measures and measurement, data collection, data analysis, and use of performance information to drive improvement.

   Performance Measurement Documents, developed by a Department of Energy special interest group.
This site has collection of performance measurement documents.

   DOE Performance Assessment & Analysis This site offers sources of DOE performance measurement documentation.

   Department of Defense, Earned Value Management
This site has information on earned value project management for government, industry and academic users.
