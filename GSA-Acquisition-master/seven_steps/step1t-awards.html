National Public Service Awards

American Society for Public Administration and the National Academy of Public Administration

Up to 5

Award, Recognition

These awards pay tribute to outstanding public service practitioners who have exhibited the highest standard of excellence, dedication, and accomplishment on a sustained basis. It also underscores the need to have creative and highly skilled individuals as career managers of complex and demanding Government functions. Award winners will be selected from all levels of public service--local, state, and Federal, as well as international and nonprofit organizations.

Nominations for the awards programs are solicited from the public service community, and may come from any source.

A cover lever must accompany the nomination from the nominator. The nomination itself may be no more than six pages (including attachments) and may be supplemented by up to five letters of recommendation
